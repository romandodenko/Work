How your Personal Information is used

  • In order to make you a registered member of our site so that you can add content to it.
  • We may use your email address to inform you about activity on our website.
  • Your IP address is recorded when performing certain actions on our website. It is never displayed publicly.

Other uses of your personal information

In addition to notifying you about activity on our site, from time to time we may contact all users via email or announcements in order to tell them any important information. You can opt out of such newsletters in your profile.

In the course of your use of our site, we may collect information that is not related to personal information about you. This data may contain technical information about the browser or the type of device you are using. This information will be used solely for the purposes of analytics and tracking the number of visitors to our site.

Ensuring data security

We strive to ensure the security of any information you provide to us. To prevent unauthorized access or disclosure of information, we have added appropriate measures and procedures to protect and ensure the security of the information we collect.
